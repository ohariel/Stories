Faux Leather Outfit

Description:

Consisting of a top, skirt and belt. Using faux leather fabric from Wherehouse Fabrics in downtown Dallas.

Originally got the idea from Jaded London, I thought to myself, I could easily make this myself! It was one of my first times working with thicker leather, it was tricky at first with testing and switching out needles and how it worked with my machine. I also struggled with ironing the fabric but figured out that it's best with overtopping it with polyester to give it more of a seamless look. After a few swatches and tries of handling the leather I got the hang of it.

In the end, I could have tweeked the skirt a little with accessorizing to make it look cleaner. Overall, happy with the look.
