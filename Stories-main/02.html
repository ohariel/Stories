Jean Outfit Set

Description:

Consisting of a top and skirt. Fabric from my closet, made out of 1 pair of jeans.

Inspired from Pinterest, I cut out different square shapes to make up a whole. It took me a while to come up with the design of the top, I wanted it to be fun and funky, so I ended up with accentuating the bust, then sewing together a belt for the waist to make it sinched.

Accessorizing is my favorite part, I included:
Ring for belt
Eyelets, Rivets
Belly chain
Spikes
